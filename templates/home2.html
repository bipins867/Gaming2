How to participate in events?

  1. Login/SignUp to your account.
  2. Go to the Dassboard.
  3. Select an Event and Click on the Participate button.
  4. Click on Pay Button,- For paying the event participating fee.
After that when you win the event. Then the winning prize will be credited to your UPI/VPA id within 24 hrs.

What is  use UPI/VPA id in this Website?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface)  / VPA ( Virtual Payment Address)  is used to send money to the user when users win any event. So from server side it is mandatory to know the upi/vpa id to send money directly to users bank account. That is why user need to enter his upi/vpa id while SignUp to his account.
